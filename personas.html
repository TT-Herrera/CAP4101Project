Persona 2

Persona Type: Adult

Name: John Jones

Age: 30

Location: Norfolk, Virignia

Technical Comfort: Moderate

Profession: Military LTJG

Back Story:
  • John lives with his wife and daughter
  • He commutes to base everyday and works long hours
  • He deploys 4-6 months out of the year and likes to spend his free time with his family
  • He loves doing interactive activities with his families and create memories
  • He has Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) due to his experience in the military
Frustations:
  • John finds loud and over overcrowded places to be overwhelming due to his PTSD
  • He does not feel comfortable to go to places that do not allow service animals or children to act freely
Motivations:
  • John enjoys being at home with his family as it is his safe place
  • He enjoys teaching his daughter to express her emotions and seeing her happy
  • He wants to teach his daughter about the aquarium and how animals or pets are a benefit to humans
Experience:
  • John likes to ensure that his family enjoys their time at the aquariums and any interactions with any animals
  • He ideally would like a good experience as he struggles on outings with his families. He does not like to show his family his struggles, as he stays strong for them
