Project Introduction

This website is set to help people with mental or medical health assistance through service animals. To help those with disabilities be able to participate in everyday life activities that are directly related to their disability. The primary functionalities that service animals can help with is guiding, alerting, stability, calming, reminding, and so much more to benefit in order to performing tasks.
